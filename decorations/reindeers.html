Reindeers

Two sustainable reindeer designs you can make from household materials.

1) Cardboard & Pistachio Shell Reindeer

Cut a reindeer silhouette from corrugated cardboard. Use painted pistachio shells or other small shells for decorative texture (antlers or body accents). Seal with a water-based varnish.

2) Toilet Paper Roll Reindeer

Use empty toilet-paper rolls as the base. Add pipe-cleaner antlers, button eyes, and a pom-pom nose. Paint or wrap in scrap fabric for color.
