Eco-Santa

A collection of sustainable Santa decorations made from recycled and repurposed materials. Ideas include cardboard Santas, bottle-cap embellishments, and fabric scraps sewn into hats.

DIY Ideas

  • Cardboard Santa: cut and paint recycled cardboard, add recycled fabric hat.
  • Jar-lid ornaments: decorate with paint and attach string.
  • Mini Santa from scrap felt and stuffing from fabric offcuts.
