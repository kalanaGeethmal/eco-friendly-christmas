Why It Matters

Over 120 million trees are cut each Christmas.
Forests support 80% of land animals. Habitat loss is devastating.
Discarded cans injure thousands of animals every year.
Recycling one can powers festive lights for 2 hours.
Origami from scrap reduces waste and carbon emissions.
